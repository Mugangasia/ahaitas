About Us

We are business advisors looking for smarter ways to implement technology solutions to real business problems.

We take our client's businesses as seriously as a key internal executive and we are equally committed to your success.

We've all been that executive – no one at ahatis is a career consultant – in mid-to large-size enterprises from CPG to Finance to Health Care and Media.

In order to provide exceptional value, the focus at ahatis is on why, not what. We look deep into the business to find the root causes of business issues because it's there that a solution will have the greatest impact. With a technology agnostic approach that adapts to our clients' needs, ahatis is expert at identifying and implementing the most cost-effective tech stack that will drive the results our clients seek.

Together, we'll find a smarter way.

Depending on your needs we will:

  • Advise when you need advice
  • Build when you need to add a custom capability
  • Implement existing solutions when they are the best option
  • Staff with people who have the skill set and experience you require
  • Create and support enterprise solutions
  • Listen to what you want and deliver what you need
  • Leave you alone when you don't need us

Timothy Weaver

With over 25 years of enterprise technology experience, Tim founded ahatis to both bring his wide ranging experience to clients and allow himself to continue to grow as a technology leader by working across a more diverse set of business challenges. He's worked with consultants in the past and was determined to create a better advisory model.

Prior to starting ahatis, Tim was Chief Information Officer for Del Monte Foods and Danone's North American businesses (Dannon, Stonyfield, Evian, and other large consumer brands), where he re-engineered a slow-moving IT function to adopt a lean and agile approach that enabled rapid implementation and analysis of innovative technologies. At Del Monte, he led one of the most aggressive SAP implementations on record, deploying the full stack of SAP business solutions in less than 9 months and rationalizing over 250 applications to the SAP footprint.

Tim has been a pioneer in the adoption of an agile, self-service Business Intelligence strategy and a predictive model-based sales planning process. At Dannon, his approach contributed to annual growth approaching 20%. During an 11+ year tenure at Red Bull, he was a key executive in opening new markets that included the USA, South Africa, Egypt, and Japan. An early member of the global technology organization, he focused on business solutions that delivered measurable, incremental top line sales growth.
